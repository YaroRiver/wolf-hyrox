# 1. WHY THIS PLAN IS BETTER

## Why not use the aggressive W22 build plan?

The master plan originally wanted W22 to target:
- WB 60 under fatigue
- BBJ 70 m
- lunges 80 m
- one sled maintenance exposure

That is still the Phase 3 direction.

But this exact week has a vaccine and a recovery debt after W21.  
Forcing full build logic now risks:

- calf/Achilles flare
- immune suppression
- poor vaccine response
- bad Thursday
- compromised Saturday
- entering W23 tired instead of sharp

## Why not use the very soft de-load plan exactly as written?

The de-load plan is safer, but it removes too much race contact:
- no sled
- no lunges
- no real station touch except WB/run
- Tuesday becomes almost too clean and too non-HYROX

That is safe, but slightly too passive.

## Final compromise

This final plan keeps the de-load structure but adds minimal race-specific contact:

- Tuesday = one controlled quality session
- tiny sled touch optional
- tiny lunge touch optional
- no BBJ Tuesday
- vaccine Wednesday
- Thursday recovery
- Saturday conditional WB ceiling session
- Sunday close-out

This keeps the week safe without losing Phase 3 direction.

# 4. READINESS GATES

## GREEN

Proceed with planned work if:

- sleep ≥ 7h30
- HRV ≥ 45 ms or within ~10–15% of baseline
- HR dip ≥ 20%
- calves / soleus / Achilles ≤ 1–2/10
- under-knee pulling = 0
- no fever
- no body aches
- no chest symptoms
- mood usable

## YELLOW

Reduce volume if:

- sleep 6h30–7h30
- HRV 40–45 ms
- HR dip 15–20%
- calves 2–3/10
- mild blocked nose only
- energy low but body okay

Rules:
- reduce volume 20–40%
- no max effort
- no extra round
- no probe
- no ego finish

## RED

Recovery only if:

- HRV < 40 ms
- HR dip < 15%
- calves ≥ 4/10
- clear under-knee pulling
- cramp signal
- fever
- body aches
- chest symptoms
- real illness feeling

Allowed:
- walk
- mobility
- easy bike only if it feels better than rest

## IMPORTANT HRV RULE

Post-nap HRV rebound clears CNS, not tissue.

Nap can upgrade:
- Red → Yellow
- Yellow → Yellow-Green

Nap cannot override:
- calf/Achilles warning
- under-knee pulling
- fever
- body aches
- vaccine reaction
